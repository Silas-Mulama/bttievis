About BTTI EVIS

Butere Technical Training Institute Electronic Voting System

Our Mission

The BTTI Electronic Voting System (EVIS) is designed to ensure **secure, transparent, and efficient elections** within the institution. It empowers students, staff, and election officials by digitizing the voting process while ensuring credibility and fairness.

Vision

To make BTTI a **leader in digital democracy**, where technology enhances trust and participation in decision-making.

Key Features

Secure Authentication

Login with admission number, password, and OTP verification for maximum security.

Real-Time Results

Votes are tallied instantly and displayed on a secure admin dashboard.

Transparency

An audit trail logs every action, ensuring transparency in elections.
